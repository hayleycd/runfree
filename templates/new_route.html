Instagram Instructions

It is easy to store routes from MapMyRun with RunFree, RunMe. When you are logged into MapMyRun, view the route that you want to store on RunFree, RunMe. There will be a link on the route page that will say "Add this route to your site". Click the link, play with the settings, and copy the embed code into the form on RunFree, RunMe.

Store your favorite routes! Store routes and attach them to your runs. They will become an important part of the visual record of your running journey.
